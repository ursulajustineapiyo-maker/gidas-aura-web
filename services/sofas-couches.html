Sofas & Couches

Your sofa is one of the most used pieces of furniture in your home and also one of the most overlooked when it comes to deep cleaning. Over time, sofas and couches absorb sweat, skin cells, pet dander, food particles, dust mites, and bacteria that regular vacuuming simply cannot remove. At Gidas Aura, we provide a thorough professional deep clean that restores your sofa to its best condition.


Our process begins with a full dry vacuum of the entire sofa cushions, armrests, backrest, and base to remove all loose dust, crumbs, and surface debris. We then carefully inspect the fabric type and identify any stains or problem areas before selecting the most suitable certified upholstery cleaning solution.


Our trained specialist applies the cleaning solution evenly and uses professional soft-bristle brushes to work it deep into the fabric fibres gently breaking down stains, odours, and embedded grime without damaging the material. For stubborn stains such as coffee, juice, grease, or pet accidents, we use targeted spot treatment before the main clean.


We then use a powerful hot water extraction machine to flush out all the cleaning solution, dissolved dirt, moisture, and allergens from deep within the fabric. This extraction method is highly effective and leaves no soap residue behind. Finally, the sofa is deodorised with a fresh, safe fragrance and left to dry in a well-ventilated space.


The result is a sofa that looks fresher, smells cleaner, and feels healthier free from allergens, bacteria, and hidden dirt. A clean sofa is not just about appearance it is about the health and comfort of everyone in your home.

Time Taken

1 to 2 hours depending on size

Result

Fresh, stain-free, allergen-free sofa
