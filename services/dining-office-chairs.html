Dining & Office Chairs

Chairs whether around your dining table or at your office desk are in constant contact with the human body every single day. They collect sweat, oils, food spills, dust, and bacteria over time, making regular professional cleaning not just a matter of appearance but a matter of hygiene and health.


At Gidas Aura, we professionally clean all types of dining and office chairs fabric, velvet, mesh, and leather using methods specifically suited to each material. We never use a one-size-fits-all approach because different fabrics require different care.


Our process starts with a thorough inspection of each chair to identify the fabric type, level of soiling, and any specific stains. For fabric and upholstered chairs, we vacuum the surfaces, apply a certified cleaning agent, scrub carefully using professional brushes, and extract all dirt and moisture using our extraction machine. For mesh office chairs, we clean each section carefully to remove trapped dust and sweat.


For leather dining and office chairs, we use a specially formulated leather cleaner that lifts dirt and grime without drying out or cracking the leather. After cleaning, we apply a premium leather conditioner that restores softness, shine, and flexibility — keeping the leather supple and extending its life significantly.


Once cleaned, every chair is deodorised and inspected before we consider the job done. The result is a set of chairs that look as close to new as possible clean, fresh, and hygienically safe for daily use.

Time Taken

30 to 45 minutes per chair

Result

Clean, refreshed, like new chairs
