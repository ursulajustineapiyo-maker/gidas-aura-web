Mattresses

We spend roughly one third of our lives sleeping yet the mattress we sleep on is one of the least cleaned items in our homes. A mattress can harbour millions of dust mites, dead skin cells, sweat residue, bacteria, and allergens that build up silently over months and years. These invisible contaminants are directly linked to poor sleep quality, allergies, skin irritation, and respiratory issues.


At Gidas Aura, our professional mattress cleaning service eliminates all of this giving you a genuinely clean, safe, and healthy sleeping surface.


Our process begins with a powerful deep vacuum of the entire mattress top, sides, and any crevices using a HEPA-filter vacuum that captures even the finest dust mite particles and allergens. We then inspect the mattress for stains such as sweat marks, blood, urine, or spills and apply targeted stain treatment solutions to break them down effectively.


A specialised anti-bacterial and anti-allergen cleaning solution is then applied evenly across the mattress surface. Our specialist works it in using soft brushes, ensuring it penetrates deep into the mattress fibres to kill bacteria and neutralise odours at the source. Our hot water extraction machine then flushes out all dissolved dirt, cleaning solution, and moisture.


The mattress is then deodorised using a safe, fresh-scented solution and left to air dry fully before being returned to use. We recommend professional mattress cleaning every 6 months for optimal health and hygiene.


The result is a mattress that is genuinely clean, sanitised, allergen-free, and fresh-smelling giving you and your family a healthier and more comfortable sleep every night.

Time Taken

1 to 1.5 hours

Result

Sanitised, allergen-free, fresh mattress
