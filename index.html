Our Story

At Gidas Aura Cleaning Services, we are committed to delivering exceptional cleaning solutions that create fresh, healthy, and welcoming spaces. Based in Nairobi, we proudly serve both residential and commercial clients with professionalism, reliability, and attention to detail.

Our services include general cleaning, deep cleaning, post-construction cleaning, office cleaning, and customized cleaning solutions tailored to meet each client's unique needs. We use safe, effective cleaning products and modern techniques to ensure high-quality results every time.

At the heart of our business is a passion for excellence and customer satisfaction. We believe that a clean space enhances comfort, productivity, and peace of mind and that's exactly what we aim to provide.

With a dedicated and trustworthy team, Gidas Aura Cleaning Services guarantees consistency, efficiency, and a sparkling finish you can count on.
